Welcome to Sideload Store, a seemless and reliable place for all your tweaked apps

If the FFapple certs have revoked on your device or you have been blacklisted, follow this tutorial to get your apps working again 1. Create an icloud backup of your device in settings. 2. Erase all contents and settings from your device by going to settings > general > reset > erase all content and settings 3. Enable FFapple.

How to set up FFapple, 1. Download DNScloak from the AppStore and the links needed to enable FFapple (sideloadstore.ga/FFapple.zip). 2. Open DNScloak and choose the "adguard-dns" server. 3. Enable blacklist by going to the setting icon in the top right and scrolling down to blacklist (make sure connect on demand is turned ON). Turn on blacklist and pick either the antirevoke or install apps file depending on your use.
